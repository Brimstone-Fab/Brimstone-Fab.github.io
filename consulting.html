Consulting

Did you just spend thousands on new welding equipment and now you're struggling to figure out why it did not magically turn your welding department into super heroes?

Are you feeling the burn of inflation on your consumable costs?

Do you think you are saving money by purchasing the cheapest welding and finishing consumables you can find?

Are you putting together a new facility and are not sure where to start?

Are you trying to boost your school's trades programs and can't seem to get the right support?

We are often promised huge quality gains with the purchase of new equipment, or by switching from this vendor to that vendor. The truth is, you can, however most of your potential gains are lost due to a disengaged vendor, complacent spending, and above all lack of or poor training. Brimstone Fabrication personnel are experts are experts in the fields of welding, production management, quality management, inspection and training. We will leverage our experience and our network of industry experts to provide you a low cost assessment. We can tour your facility or discuss your upcoming projects and present you cost savings options and training oppurtunities so your team can start achieving the lean goals you had hoped for. We at Brimstone believe in helping our manufacturing partners rather than competing with them, so much so that we back our consultation services with a money back guarantee. If Brimstone can not find an avenue of cost savings for you and your team, we will not invoice for our services, plain and simple. Simply fill out our short questionnaire and one of our specialists will quickly reach out to you for a follow up discussion.
